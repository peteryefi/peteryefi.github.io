I'm a Visionary Technology and Product Leader with 8+ years of experience engineering cloud-native ecosystems and directing cross-functional teams of 15+. I specialize in managing Product, Design, DevOps, and QA to transform complex requirements into scalable architectures.

I hold a PhD in Software Engineering from Concordia University, where my research focused on metamodeling for IoT and building management systems — enabling interoperability across mechanical, electrical, and plumbing (MEP) systems in smart buildings.
